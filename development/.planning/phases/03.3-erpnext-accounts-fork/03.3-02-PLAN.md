<objective>
Fork the 5 ERPNext controller files into university_finance/controllers/, strip all stock/selling/buying dependencies from AccountsController, rewrite Sales Invoice and Purchase Invoice controllers to inherit from AccountsController instead of SellingController/BuyingController, and archive 56 unused doctype directories to _archived/.

Purpose: The controller hierarchy (StatusUpdater -> TransactionBase -> AccountsController) is the backbone of all financial document processing. Without forking these controllers and stripping ERPNext stock/selling/buying dependencies, no financial doctype (Payment Entry, Journal Entry, etc.) can function independently of ERPNext. The archive step reduces noise for maintenance.

Output: Fully independent controller hierarchy under university_finance/controllers/ with zero erpnext imports. 56 archived doctypes moved out of active directory.
</objective>
